Lil Wayne - The GOAT

Lil Wayne, born Dwayne Michael Carter Jr., is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record executive. He is considered by many to be one of the greatest rappers of all time, with a career spanning over two decades. Wayne has released 13 studio albums, including the critically acclaimed "The Carter III" and "The Carter IV". He has won five Grammy Awards and various others within the span of his career. Lil Wayne has been instrumental in discovering and nurturing some of the biggest names in hip hop. He discovered Drake in 2008 and signed him to his label Young Money Entertainment. Drake’s debut album, “Thank Me Later,” was released in 2010 and went on to become a commercial and critical success. Wayne also discovered Nicki Minaj in 2009 and signed her to Young Money Entertainment. Minaj’s debut album, “Pink Friday,” was released in 2010 and was also a commercial and critical success. Both Drake and Minaj have gone on to become two of the biggest names in hip hop, with numerous hit songs and awards to their names.
