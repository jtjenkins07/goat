Lil Wayne: The GOAT

Lil Wayne is a Grammy-winning rapper from New Orleans, Louisiana. He is considered one of the greatest rappers of all time.

He is known for his unique style, wordplay, and metaphors. He has released 13 studio albums and has sold over 120 million records worldwide.
