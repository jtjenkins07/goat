Lil Wayne - The GOAT

Lil Wayne, born Dwayne Michael Carter Jr., is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record executive. He is considered by many to be one of the greatest rappers of all time, with a career spanning over two decades. Wayne has released 13 studio albums, including the critically acclaimed "Tha Carter III" and "Tha Carter IV". He has won five Grammy Awards, 11 BET Awards, and four Billboard Music Awards, among others.

Here's a quote from Lil Wayne himself:

"I'm the best rapper alive, and I'll prove it any time, anywhere."
